About Scripts

Scripts is an application targeted towards getting rid of the phenomena of “Slacktivism”. Slacktivism is a combination of the words “slacker” and “activism” which has increasingly been used to describe the disconnect between awareness and action through the use of social media.

Why Scripts?

Petition creators develop a template for users to be able to know exactly what to say to an issue target. Scripts doesn’t just ask the user to take action, it guides them through a template and makes the phone number of the issue target accessible. Signers of a petition can also see what other users have to say about the issue that they care about.
